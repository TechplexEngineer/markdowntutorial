Emphasis

Text wrapped inside asterisks (*) and underscores (_) will be emphasized. Use the one you prefer.

You can use bold and italic in the same text at the same time. For this exercise, make the words "The New York Times Best Seller" bold and "The New York Times" italic. Hint: You can also use asterisks and underscores at the same time.

If you need to use an asterisk or an underscore as a literal instead of as delimiter, use a backslash to escape it. In this exercise, convert the text 20% OFF (in italics) to *20% OFF* (surrounded by asterisks).
